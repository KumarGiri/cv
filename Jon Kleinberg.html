I am a professor a Cornell University. My research focuses on issuess a the interface of networks and information, with an emphasis on the social and information networks that underpin the Web and other on-line media. My work has been supported by an NSF Career Award, an ONR Yound Investfator Award, a MacArthur Foundation Fellowship, a Packard Foundation Fellowship, a Simon Investifator Award, a Sloan Foundation Fellowship, and grants from Facebook, Google, Yahoo, the ARO, and the NSF. I am a member of the National Academy of Sciences, the National Academy of Engineering, and theAmerican Academy of Arts and Sciences.
